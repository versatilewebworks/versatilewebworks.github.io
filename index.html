About

Crafting interfaces that
feel as good as they look.

I'm a freelance UI/UX designer and frontend developer with over two years of hands-on experience turning complex product requirements into clean, intuitive digital experiences. My work lives precisely at the intersection of visual design and functional code — I don't just hand off mockups, I build them.

Specialising in Figma-to-code workflows, I design fully componentised systems in Figma — complete with variables, auto-layout, and interactive prototypes — then bring them to life with semantic HTML, modern CSS, and JavaScript. Every pixel is intentional, every interaction considered.

My clients are early-stage founders, growing SaaS teams, and agencies who need design that converts visitors, retains users, and scales gracefully. I care about accessibility, performance, and the tiny details that separate a good product from one people genuinely love to use.
