About Me

I design interfaces that
actually convert.

I'm a freelance UI designer and frontend developer with five years of experience building digital products for startups and growing brands. My work lives at the intersection of beautiful design and clean, performant code.

I specialise in Figma-to-code workflows — designing in Figma, then bringing it to life with semantic HTML, modern CSS, and vanilla JavaScript (or React when the project calls for it).

My clients are founders, product teams, and agencies who want design that looks deliberate, converts visitors, and holds up under real-world conditions. I care about the details that make the difference between good and great.
